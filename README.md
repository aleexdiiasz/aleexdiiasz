## About Me

I am a **Computer Systems Engineer** and **Full Stack Developer** with more than four years of experience building enterprise software, backend services and business automation solutions.

My core specialization is backend engineering with **C#**, **ASP.NET Core**, **Entity Framework Core** and scalable **RESTful APIs**. I also build modern web interfaces with **React**, **Laravel** and **Blazor WebAssembly**.

I currently participate in the development of a cloud-based, multi-tenant SaaS platform for the notarial sector. My work combines software architecture, product engineering, cloud infrastructure and operational automation.

I approach engineering as a product discipline: understand the business problem, design a maintainable solution, protect critical data and deliver measurable operational value.

```text
Backend Engineering     ASP.NET Core, C#, REST APIs, Entity Framework Core

```
